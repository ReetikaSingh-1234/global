About Us

We are not a staffing firm, We are team of expert technical resources who provide us the right filtered candidates. We have our own AI enabled database which brings closely mapped candidates for your requirement with a quick turn around time.

Global Hunt Solutions Started in 2023 with the mindset to provide right employee to right employer. We have placed 500+candidates till now. We have been actively placing candidates in tech & non tech domain.

We have worked in major below industries: Banking & Finance, Fintech, E- learning, E Commerce, Digital Marketing, Software Development, IT Services, Infrastructure, Automobile and many more.
